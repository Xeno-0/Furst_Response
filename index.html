Pet Health Tips

Essential advice to keep your dog healthy and happy

Nutrition Essentials

Feed your dog high-quality protein sources and ensure they have access to fresh water at all times. Avoid foods toxic to dogs like chocolate, grapes, and onions.

Exercise Requirements

Most dogs need at least 30-60 minutes of exercise daily. Adjust based on breed, age, and health. Regular walks prevent obesity and promote cardiovascular health.

Vaccination Schedule

Keep vaccinations up to date. Core vaccines include rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and adenovirus. Consult your vet for a personalized schedule.
